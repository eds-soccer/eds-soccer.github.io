SUMMER CAMPS

LEARN TO PLAY THE EUROPEAN WAY.


At E.D.S Soccer, our team of professional coaches from Scotland and across Europe offer a fantastic learning experience for those just beginning their soccer journey and also for those who are more experienced and playing regularly for teams. Each day will focus on certain aspects of the game. By the end of the week we can guarantee that players will have learned something valuable and most of all developed a passion for the sport.

Our Summer Camps are targeted at children of all abilities aged between 5 and 17. Our carefully and thoroughly designed curriculum ensures participants are coached in an exciting and challenging environment.

Our ENGAGING sessions are designed to DEVELOP and SHAPE individual ability, teamwork, game awareness and physicality.

As well as gaining valuable experience and skills required for soccer - participants will also DEVELOP character, charisma and communication skills on top of other invaluable attributes that SHAPE confident individuals, respectful citizens, effective contributors and successful learners.

All campers will receive EDS Soccer branded merchandise.
