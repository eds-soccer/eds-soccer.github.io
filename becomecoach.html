How would you like to live and work in the Bay Area of California this coming Summer?

You can do so by applying to work with EDS Soccer.
To become a coach with EDS Soccer simply email your CV and a covering letter HERE.


What does the role involve?

  1. Camp coaches are expected to work between 15-30 hours per week
  2. This will be spread across morning camps, day camps and evening and weekend clinics
  3. The majority of working hours are completed Monday-Friday with rotational work at weekends
  4. Coaches are expected to lead groups of 10-20 children with opportunities to also direct camps
  5. Please note that every coach must pay a fee to participate in the Summer program and is responsible for all cost towards, flights, VISA and insurance


What We Offer

  1. 2 day training course before departure to California
  2. Shared accommodation with fellow coaches located only 30mins by train (BART) to San Francisco
  3. Competitive pay
  4. Cars available for travel if there is no business need
  5. An experience to remember in the Golden State!
  6. Potential of end of summer trip (performance based)

What We Are Looking For

  1. Minimum Qualification SFA level 1.2
  2. Experience of working with children aged 4-17s in a football/soccer environment
  3. A passion for football/soccer
  4. Studying towards or already completed a degree in childcare, primary/secondary teaching, sports coaching or PE qualification
  5. Leadership and organisational skills
  6. Vibrant, fun, confident personality
  7. Commitment to high quality customer service to parents and children
  8. Team player with a willingness to adapt


Desirable

  1. Strong background in playing or officiating sport
  2. A valid First Aid qualification
