Clinics

EDS Soccer's 'Element Specific Clinic' are tailored to allow those who are currently playing in the game to work on specific position focused skills. Our team of professional coaches have benefitted of playing at a high level and therefore have been coached personally by top professionals. This has allowed our coaches to design intense game like exercises working on aspects such as;

  1. Movement and Finishing (with Goalkeeping Focus)
  2. Passing and Crossing
  3. Ball Control
  4. Attacking and Defending

Again, our tailored clinics maximise time on the ball, movement and fun.
