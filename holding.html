• Enage, Develop, Shape

EDS Soccer stands for Engage, Develop, Shape - our ethos and philosophy when it comes to coaching soccer players. Our aim is to provide professional coaching to children as young as four up to late teens and of all abilities. We believe that all children deserve the chance to experience fun and challenging practices that not only develop them as soccer players but also as people.

We intend to create an environment where children can enjoy the sport as well as challenge themselves. To do this we have designed a curriculum that ensures children are given maximum time with a ball at their feet and limited time standing still.

Our practices are prepared to bring out the best in each child, giving each performer a chance to express themselves and learn progressively.

EDS Soccer aims to provide summer camps to children in Northern California, as well as clinics and 1-2-1 sessions. Our Summer Camps will be a mixture of morning camps and evening camps that will give children an opportunity to learn and develop key skills such as passing, dribbling and shooting, all of which are needed to be successful in soccer. Clinics will be schedule during the evening and weekends and offer players the platform to learn the skills needed to play in specific positions such as a goalkeeper or striker. 1-2-1 sessions are offered to any child wishing to learn and develop skills out with camps and clinics. These offer a more personal experience and can be booked as individuals or in small groups.

All our coaches are SFA/UEFA qualified with vast experience in coaching youth soccer players both in North America and Europe.
